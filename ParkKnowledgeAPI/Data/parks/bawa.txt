Baltimore-Washington Parkway
State(s): MD

Description:
This 29-mile highway connects Baltimore, MD and Washington, D.C. The parkway has carried visitors to and from the capital city since 1954.

Directions:
This 29-mile highway connects Baltimore, MD and Washington, D.C

Operating Hours:
Baltimore-Washington Parkway: The Baltimore-Washington Parkway is generally open year round, 24 hours a day. Check current conditions for information about closures due to road work and inclement weather.
