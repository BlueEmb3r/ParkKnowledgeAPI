Greenbelt Park
State(s): MD

Description:
Enjoy affordable camping, hiking, and peaceful forest surroundings just ten miles from Washington, DC!

Directions:
Off I-95 South Take Exit 23 Route 201 Kenilworth Avenue South(toward Bladensburg). (this will be a left). Drive on Kenilworth Avenue (Route 201). Once crossing over I-95, you will see sign for Route 193 Greenbelt Road. Veer right to Take Route 193 East. Take a left on Greenbelt Road . (Greenbelt Road is Route 193 and is an overpass over Route 201) The park entrance is a quarter mile on the right hand side.

Operating Hours:
Greenbelt Park: Open daily from 6:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m.
Good Luck Road Parking Area: Open daily for vehicle access from 7:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m., with pedestrian access allowed from 6:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. Gates are closed on Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Day, and New Year’s Day.
Sweetgum Picnic Area Gates: Sweetgum Picnic Area is open daily from 6:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. for pedestrian access. The gates open to vehicles from 9:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. and are closed on Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Day, and New Year’s Day.
