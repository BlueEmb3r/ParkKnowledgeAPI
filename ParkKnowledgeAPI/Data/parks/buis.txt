Buck Island Reef National Monument
State(s): VI

Description:
Welcome to Buck Island Reef National Monument, called “the finest marine garden in the Caribbean Sea” by President John F. Kennedy. Snorkel the famous underwater trail, explore endangered elkhorn coral reefs. Hike the trails on the 176-acre island to the observation platform for an unparallel view. Discover a vibrant world above and beneath the waves in this Caribbean paradise.

Directions:
Use one of our concessionaires to get to Buck Island!

Operating Hours:
Buck Island Reef National Monument: Buck Island Reef National Monument is open year-round, every day of the week. The current operating hours are 7:00 am to 5:00 pm.

There is no overnight camping on the island; the park is closed at night to protect nesting sea turtles.

Camping is not allowed on the island.
